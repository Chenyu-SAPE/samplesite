The Dangers of Screen Addiction

As a parent, finding the perfect gift that’s both fun and educational can be a challenge. Like finding a movie that’s actually “fun for the whole family.”

My kids are perpetually fixated on finding their next favorite toy, and I’m always looking for something entertaining and mentally stimulating. Too much screen time, or at least the effects of it, occasionally turn my sweet little angels into screaming, leaky hobgoblins.

Because of the mood swings, the headaches from eye strain, and shortened temper, I could see how screens were affecting my kids. Their little bodies are brimming with energy to burn, and they’re not meant to sit in one place and drool for three hours.
